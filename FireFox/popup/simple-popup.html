Ranobe Gemini

Processes large chapters in parts to avoid token limits

Ranobe Gemini v1.0

This extension uses Google's Gemini AI to enhance novel translations, making them more readable and natural.

You need a valid Gemini API key to use this extension. Get a free key

Usage:

  1. Navigate to a novel chapter
  2. Click the "Enhance with Gemini" button on the page
  3. The enhanced text will replace the original content
