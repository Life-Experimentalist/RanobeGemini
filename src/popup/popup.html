⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Default shortcuts: Alt+Shift+L for Library, Alt+Shift+E for Enhance, and Alt+Shift+S for Summary.
To change them, open your browser's extension shortcut manager. Chromium browsers use chrome://extensions/shortcuts or edge://extensions/shortcuts. Firefox manages this from the add-on settings page.

📑 Content Sections

Collapsible blocks injected by Gemini into enhanced chapters. Each disabled section shows content inline. Collapsed sections are read as summaries by the TTS/read-aloud feature when available.
Identifies combat sequences ≥3 paragraphs and wraps them with a summary.
Wraps explicit adult scenes with a tasteful summary.
Notes >150 words or about release schedules/personal life are automatically collapsed with a story-relevant summary.
Custom types need Gemini instructions. Tell Gemini (via permanent prompt or custom prompt) to wrap content in: <div class="rg-collapsible-section" data-type="YOUR_ID" data-name="Name" data-summary="…">
