Ranobe Gemini Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 6, 2025

Data we do not collect

Ranobe Gemini does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. There are no analytics, trackers, or ads in the extension or this site.

Local processing

All enhancement and summarization happens locally in your browser. Requests to Gemini or your selected model use the API key you provide; keys are stored only in your browser storage.

Google Drive backups

  • Backups are optional and use your own Google Drive account.
  • OAuth is performed via Google; tokens are stored in your extension storage.
  • No backup data is sent to third-party servers owned by us.

Permissions

  • activeTab / tabs: to inject UI on supported sites.
  • storage: to keep your settings, prompts, and library.
  • identity: to complete Google OAuth when you opt into Drive backups.
  • downloads: to export local backups when you trigger them.

Data retention

All extension data lives in your browser profile until you clear it. Drive backups stay in your Drive until you delete them.

Your controls

  • Clear tokens: use “Reset Drive Auth” in the popup to remove OAuth tokens.
  • Remove data: clear extension storage via your browser or uninstall the extension.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
