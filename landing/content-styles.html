🎨 Content Styles & Filters

Complete control over how your chapters are displayed. Ranobe Gemini uses intelligent content detection and user-defined filters to help you read comfortably and at your own pace.

📦 Built-in Content Box Types

Gemini automatically detects and marks these content types during chapter enhancement. Each has a distinct visual style for easy recognition.

📊
Game Stats

RPG/LitRPG stat windows, character sheets, and numerical data formatted for readability.

🔔
System Messages

In-world notifications, alerts, and system prompts with distinct visual formatting.

⚡
Skill Cards

Individual ability descriptions, spell details, and skill acquisition announcements.

📝
Author Notes

Long author commentary, translator notes, and off-topic messages styled distinctly.

💬
Quotes & Poetry

Epigraphs, character quotes, song lyrics, and poetic passages with italic styling.

⏮️
Flashbacks

Memory inserts, past event recollections, and timeline shifts marked with dashed borders.

🔽 Collapsible Content Filters

Instead of permanently hiding content, Ranobe Gemini lets you collapse sections so you can expand them whenever you want. Collapse is temporary and user-controlled.

💡 Key Concept: Collapsible ≠ Hidden. We make content accessible while keeping your reading flow smooth. You're always in control.

Three Built-in Filter Types

⚔️ Fight Scenes

Action sequences, combat details, and battle mechanics. Collapsed by default because detailed fighting can interrupt story pacing. Click to expand when you're ready for the action.

🔞 Mature Content

Explicit romantic or adult scenes. Collapsed by default so you can read comfortably in public or around others. Summaries preserve story context (relationships, emotional beats) without full details.

📝 Author's Notes

Long, tangential author commentary. Collapsed by default to preserve reading flow. Story-relevant notes (character clarifications, translation notes) always remain visible.

How It Works

⚙️ Configure Your Filters

Access your filter settings from the library. You have complete control over which content types are collapsed, expanded, or disabled.

Available Controls

  • ✓
    Toggle Each Type – Enable or disable fight scenes, R18 content, author notes. Disabled types appear inline without collapse boxes.
  • ⛔
    Set Default State – Choose whether each type starts collapsed or expanded. Users can toggle them individually while reading.
  • ➕
    Create Custom Types – Define your own collapsible categories like "Flashbacks," "World Exposition," "Character development," etc.
  • 🧪
    Test Before Applying – Use the playground in settings to try configurations on real chapter content before saving.
  • 💾
    Settings Persist – Your filter preferences are saved locally in your browser. Same settings apply to every chapter you read.

🔗 Integration Points

Content filters are integrated throughout Ranobe Gemini:

📖 Chapter Reading

Collapsible sections appear automatically in every chapter you read. Gemini AI detects the content types and marks them accordingly.

🔊 Text-to-Speech (TTS)

When you use the read-aloud feature, collapsed sections are skipped by default (unless you expand them first). Expanded sections are read normally.

📚 Library Settings

Go to your Library to configure all filter settings, test them in the playground, and manage custom content types.

💾 Persistence

Your filter preferences are saved locally in your browser. Settings apply consistently across all chapters.
